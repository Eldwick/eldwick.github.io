I'm James and I build unique solutions for untapped opportunities.

My core motivation is to create meaningful change. Beginning in politics and advocacy I was able to achieve some signiciant success, but I came to realize that I could have a bigger impact in the private sector - especially in tech.

I discovered my passion for programming in a sales role at an early stage start-up. Out of curiousity and the pain of manually recording data, I started to teach myself some javascript to help automate a few tasks. What began as the equvalent of simple VBA scripts grew into a full CRM tool that tracked thousands of calls, collected new valuble metrics, and assisted in hundreds of sales.
